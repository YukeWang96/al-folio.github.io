Yuke Wang

Santa Barbara, CA, USA

Yuke Wang is a fourth-year Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in computer science at University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB). He got his Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in software engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2018.

At UCSB, Yuke is working with Prof Yufei Ding. Yuke's research interests include high-performance computing and deep learning algorithms. His recent ongoing projects cover graph neural network (GNN) optimization and its acceleration on GPUs.
